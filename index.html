Overview

Welcome to the portfolio website for Daniel McGinn! I'm a design enthusiast with a passion for solving complex problems. I studied Mechanical Engineering at Tufts University and I work as a Product Manager at SolidWorks. When I'm not immersed in the world of design, you'll find me on adventures in the outdoors.

As a PM at SolidWorks, I manage a portfolio of cloud-based CAD apps. I've designed diverse datasets and prepared engaging demos to showcase proper design practices for parts, assemblies, surfacing, frame design, sheet metal design, and drawings.
